Mission

Cloud Hero AI exists to give every cloud engineering team AI-native tools that actually work — not AI bolted onto legacy SaaS. Tools that speak the language of infrastructure, understand cost tradeoffs, and make engineers more powerful, not more dependent on vendors.

We believe the cloud cost crisis is a tooling problem. $400 billion in cloud spend is wasted annually — not because engineers are careless, but because the tooling to prevent waste didn't exist. We're building it.

The Problem

The state of cloud
tooling is broken.

Cloud teams in 2026 manage orders of magnitude more complexity than they did five years ago — multi-cloud environments, AI workloads, microservice architectures, thousands of infrastructure resources. But the tools they use haven't kept pace.

The result is a silent crisis: engineers waste hours on repetitive work, FinOps teams fight spreadsheet battles to find waste, and cloud bills grow 30–40% year-over-year with no clear line of sight into why.

Our Answer

We're fixing it
with AI.

Cloud Hero AI is a four-product platform where each product targets a specific failure mode in cloud team operations. Together, they form a flywheel that makes cloud teams measurably better over time.

The key insight: AI doesn't just automate tasks — it changes what's possible. A task that took a senior engineer 4 hours now takes a junior engineer 4 minutes. That changes the economics of cloud operations entirely.

  • Hero Savings eliminates the manual cloud audit process — permanently
  • Hero Agents replaces repetitive multi-step workflows with intelligent automations
  • Hero Copilot gives every engineer on-demand access to senior DevOps expertise
  • Hero Weekly keeps the entire team current on a fast-moving landscape
