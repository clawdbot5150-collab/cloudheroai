What We Find

Six categories of waste.
One-click fixes for all of them.

Hero Savings finds every type of cloud waste — not just the obvious stuff. Our AI catches what humans miss.

🖥️

Idle EC2 Instances

Instances with CPU utilization under 5% for the past 14 days. Dev boxes that nobody turned off. Forgotten staging environments running 24/7.

Avg monthly savings $2,100/mo
💾

Unattached EBS Volumes

Storage volumes left behind when instances are terminated. Pay for storage that's connected to nothing and accessed by no one.

Avg monthly savings $640/mo
🗄️

Oversized RDS Clusters

Database clusters provisioned for peak load that's never happened. Multi-AZ setups where a single instance would do. db.r6g.4xlarge running at 8% memory utilization.

Avg monthly savings $1,800/mo
📸

Old Snapshots & AMIs

Automated snapshots from 18 months ago still sitting in S3. AMIs from builds that shipped and shipped again three major versions ago.

Avg monthly savings $380/mo
🌐

Unused Elastic IPs

Reserved but unattached Elastic IP addresses. AWS charges $0.005/hour whether they're doing anything or not. Small amounts that add up across a fleet.

Avg monthly savings $110/mo
🔀

Oversized NAT Gateways

NAT Gateways with minimal throughput that could be consolidated. Data transfer charges from poorly architected traffic routing patterns.

Avg monthly savings $890/mo
