Hero Savings — Performance Fee

We only charge 15% of your verified monthly cloud savings. No flat fee, no upfront cost. We don't make money until you do.

15% of verified savings
No upfront cost · Cancel anytime
How your fee is calculated: Each month we re-scan your cloud accounts and compare against your 90-day baseline. We only charge on savings above $100/mo. Example: if your baseline was $25,000/mo and your bill drops to $17,750 — your verified savings are $7,250 and your fee is $1,088 (15%). You keep $6,162. That's a 5.7× return. Fees are billed monthly via Stripe metered billing.
